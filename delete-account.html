Delete Your BikerZone Account

To request deletion of your BikerZone account and associated data, send an email to:

bikerzone.app@gmail.com

Include the email address used to register your account.

Data Deleted

  • User profile information
  • Uploaded images
  • Posts and comments
  • Authentication data associated with the account

Retention

Account and associated data are deleted within 30 days of verification of the request.
